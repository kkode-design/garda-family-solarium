Privacy Policy of Garda Family & Solarium

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Garda Family & Solarium collects, uses, and protects the personal data of users and guests. By using our website or booking a stay, you accept the practices described in this policy.

2. Data Collected

  • Personal Data: First name, last name, email address, phone number, and payment details.
  • Booking Data: Stay dates, booked apartment, and number of guests.
  • Browsing Data: Information collected through cookies and tracking technologies to enhance the user experience.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

  • To process bookings and provide the requested services.
  • To contact guests regarding their booking.
  • To improve the website experience through cookies.

4. Data Sharing

Personal data will not be shared with third parties, except when required by law or necessary for contract execution (e.g., payment services).

5. Data Protection

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect users' personal data.

6. User Rights

  • The right to access, rectify, delete, and object to the processing of personal data.
  • Users can exercise their rights by contacting us at the email address provided in the "Contacts" section.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

Garda Family & Solarium reserves the right to modify this policy. Users will be informed of any significant changes.

8. Contacts

For any questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the "Contact Us" section on our website or send an email to the address provided in the booking confirmation.

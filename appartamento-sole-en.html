Other information

The building's gate and our garage (available upon reservation, subject to availability, at a cost of 10 euros per night) offer security, independence, and privacy.

These are further ensured by the option for self-check-in and the presence of some external security cameras. The pedestrian entrance to the property is through an exclusive entry, separate from the two private residences within the same building.

Our large rooftop sun terrace is equipped with sunbeds, an electric barbecue, and a hot tub (open from mid-May to mid-October), allowing you to enjoy a stay centered around relaxation and tranquility!

We kindly ask guests who have not reserved a parking spot or bike space in the garage, after receiving the keys and unloading their luggage, to please move their car to the large free parking area on Via Italo Marchi, as parking in the courtyard is unfortunately not allowed. Thank you, and we look forward to welcoming you!
